Yummy Yummy Burger

Very Delish Looking Burger!!!

Ingredients:

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs
  • Burger buns
  • Sliced circular tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Pickles
  • Square cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat your cooking surface and lightly coat in cooking oil
  2. Whisk the egg, salt, and pepper in a bowl. Add Beef and bread crumbs, then mix with hands or fork until it is blended well.
  3. Form the meat into 3/4 inch thick patties
  4. Place patties onto the cooking surface and cook for 6-8 minutes per side. The center of the meat should be 160 degrees F.
  5. Now, to build the burger.

How to Build the Perfect burger

  1. bottom of burger bun
  2. sauce (your choice)
  3. Patty
  4. cheese
  5. tomato
  6. lettuce
  7. pickles
