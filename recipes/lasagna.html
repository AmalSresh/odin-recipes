World's Best Lasagna

Most delicious looking lasagna!!! OOOOH!

Ingredients:

  • Sweet Italian sausage and ground beef
  • 1 onion, 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes, 2 cans of tomato sauce, 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • Parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook Noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna - Check the Next Section
  5. Cover with foil and bake. 375 degrees and 25 minutes with foil on, then 25 minutes with the foil off.

How to layer lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan.
